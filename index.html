The beginning

It was a snowy day in December of 2014 in Burlington, VT. I (Abbi) hauled myself over about a foot of snow with the crisp wind blowing in my face for about 1 mile from my apartment to get to Duine Duende, an American style restaurant. There I met a lanky, tall guy who shyly smiled and introduced himself. We awkwardly made our way to the dinner table and sat down- silent- for about 10 minutes. After the food came out- delicious chicken and waffles might I add-though we talked a little bit more (thankfully haha) and walked out of the restaurant an hour later with smiles on our faces. We hugged with a casual see you again sometime soon (which happened to be 3 weeks later at the movies) and went our separate ways...
