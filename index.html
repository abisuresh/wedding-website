Brian's story: Brian is...

The beginning:

It was a snowy day in December of 2014 in Burlington, VT. I (Abbi) hauled myself over about a foot of snow with the crisp wind blowing in my face for about 1 mile from my apartment to get to Duine Duende, an American style restaurant. There I met a lanky, tall guy who shyly smiled and introduced himself. We awkwardly made our way to the dinner table and sat down- silent- for about 10 minutes. I found out- quite quickly- that I was the chatterbox compared to Brian and if I wanted to have a conversation I was going to have to say something first haha. After the food came out- delicious chicken and waffles might I add-though we talked a little bit more and walked out of the restaurant an hour later with smiles on our faces. I have to say from the start I felt like I had met someone special. From then on we met up quite regularly and in about 3 months had become fairly serious in our relationship.
